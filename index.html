Our Story

In our community, many youth lack access to basic resources, tutoring, and support. Inspired to uplift them, we founded Caring Hands of Tennessee. Our mission is to ensure access to healthcare, education, and financial literacy so every young person can thrive.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To expand access to healthcare, financial literacy, and education, empowering underserved communities to thrive.

Vision

To create a future where underserved youth thrive with health, knowledge, and opportunity.

What We Do

Education Support

Tutoring, mentoring, help with school resources, study tools, and more.

Healthcare Access

Connecting youth and families with health resources, wellness checks, prevention programs, and support.

Financial Literacy

Workshops and coaching for budgeting, saving, financial planning, and understanding money.
