Our Story

In our community, many youth lack access to basic resources, tutoring, and support. Inspired to uplift them, we launched programs in education, healthcare, and financial literacy. Caring Hands of Tennessee was created to ensure everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To expand access to healthcare, financial literacy, and education, empowering underserved communities to thrive.

Vision

To create a future where underserved youth thrive with health, knowledge, and opportunity.

Tutoring Programs

We offer tutoring in all English courses, all sciences, all math up to Calculus, and ACT preparation. Our goal is to help students build confidence and succeed academically.
