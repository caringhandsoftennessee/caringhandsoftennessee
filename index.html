Empowering Our Community Through Education & Health

Providing access, support, and opportunity to every underserved youth.

Mission

To expand access to healthcare, financial literacy, and education, empowering underserved communities to thrive.

Vision

To create a future where underserved youth thrive with health, knowledge, and opportunity.

Our Story

In our community, many youth lack access to basic resources, tutoring, and support. Inspired to uplift them, we launched programs in education, health, and financial literacy to ensure that no one is left behind.

Caring Hands of Tennessee was created with a simple belief: everyone deserves unlimited access to education and healthcare. What began as a vision to serve local youth has grown into a mission to empower families, strengthen communities, and provide resources that create lasting change.

Programs

We provide tutoring, mentorship, and resources to help students succeed academically and build confidence.

We offer wellness workshops, guidance, and support services to ensure youth have access to health resources.

Workshops and guidance help youth and families develop budgeting and money management skills.
